The algebra of all bounded operator $\mathcal{B}(\mathcal{H})$ on a Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$ is a $C^*$ algebra.

>Operator $A: V\rightarrow W$ is called bounded if
>$$
>\sup_{f\in V}\frac{||Af||_W}{||f||_V}=\sup_{||f||_ V=1}||Af||_W<\infty
>$$
